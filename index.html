Andheri West, had the opportunity to design a dance studio for a professional dance choreographer. The client envisioned a unique 360-degree black studio, complete with black flooring and ceiling, as well as a space featuring four distinct walls that harmoniously complement each other. To meet these requirements, I created a dynamic environment with a variety of textures and colors: one wall showcases a concrete brick texture, another features white MDF panel grooving, a third is adorned with teal blue wallpaper, and the fourth wall serves as a striking mirror wall. The 360-degree black studio is enhanced by a black grid ceiling, black vinyl flooring, and curtains on all four walls, resulting in a stunning and cohesive design. The final outcome not only fulfills the client's vision but also creates an inspiring space for dance, leading to complete satisfaction with the design

Our Process

BRIEFING

We begin with an exhaustive questionnaire to fully understand the client’s needs and expectations. The most important of all steps.

Translating the brief into the blueprint of what is to come. From moodboards to models, we design every last detail.

Bringing our designs to life by building it from the ground up. As we only take on end-to-end projects, everything from structural design to decor is executed in this phase.

The most anticipated moment, where we hand our clients the keys to their new space, all ready to move in.
